Why Programming is Hard?

Programming is hard by it's nature but when you make the commitment this becomes a Play

Have you ever found that learning something is difficult in your life? Have you ever wondered why? Then let us investigate why. Simply by observing Why is it easier to play a game? rather than learning something new. Because it creates a particular chemistry in the body and psyche. This specific chemistry establishes a pattern of happiness in the system, which is why things get easier mentally. The same phenomenon happens after five hours of viewing a movie. You already know every aspect in the movie, you waste no time, and you absorb all of the information in the movie.

Watching a certain movie for five hours seems so fast while learning/studying something in hour seems a day.

Now, let's get back to Programming programming is hard by it's nature but when you make the commitment that whatever happens you should do it and learn it. First step is you need to create this certain chemistry that you want to learn.
